TRUNDLE TRAY™ - UTE STORAGE DRAWER

The original and the best under tray storage drawer on the market - developed in 1992. The Tip Top Trundle Tray™ is design to optimise the storage capacity of your ute and provides the ideal solution to secure longer tools such levels, crowbars, shovels and screeds. Beware of inferior copies.

Tip Top Trundle Tray’s™ are constructed of galvanised steel material, have an extruded aluminium face for strength and waterproofing. The extruded face comes with a finger pull, for ease, when opening the roll out drawer. This ensures our trundle drawer is long lasting and durable.

The Trundle Tray™ Storage Drawer also has sealed rollers ensuring durability, 3 point key locking for security and heavy duty rubber bump stops for added safety.
