A Global Company with Family Values

Since 1969, Magnattack® has been a trusted global leader in premium magnetic separator equipment and solutions, specializing in foreign metal fragment control for the food processing industry and beyond. In 2001, we became the first magnetic separator manufacturer worldwide to receive HACCP International certification, demonstrating our commitment to food safety excellence. Guided by core values like customer-first service, reliability, and humility, we deliver innovative systems designed to enhance safety, quality, and efficiency. Our mission is to empower industries with advanced solutions that protect products, brands, and communities while striving to create a safer future. With nearly six decades of expertise and a commitment to continuous improvement, Magnattack sets the standard for magnetic separation worldwide.
