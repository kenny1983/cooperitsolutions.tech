Acknowledgement Of Country

The nib Newcastle Knights respectfully acknowledge the Awabakal and Worimi people as the traditional owners of the lands on which we live, work and play, and we pay our respects to Elders past, present and emerging. Furthermore, the Knights and the Wests Group Australia acknowledge and are proud to represent the Nations of our region and beyond, including the Darkinjung, Wonnarua, Geawegal, Biripi, Wiradjuri, Gamilaroi, Anaiwan, Gumbaynggirr, Bundjalung, and Ngarabal Nations.
